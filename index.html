Welcome on the website about great sports game Squash

Rules

Rules are simple as they only can be:
1. Hit rubber ball over the wall
2. Do not let the ball bounce more than one time on the ground
3. Do not get hit by the ball
4. Survive!

Equipment

All you need is a sports racket, sportswear and willingness to fight
